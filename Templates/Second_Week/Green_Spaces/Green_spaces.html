GREEN SPACES INC.

WHAT WE DO

Green Spaces Inc. was originally founded in 2001 as a conifer and decidous tree nursery. Green Spaces inc started as a local tree nursery serving a limited geographic area. Our company has evolved and responded to our customers demand of quality nursery products. We continue to improve our on-line capabilities and expand our product offerings.

OUR PRODUCTS

We have extended our offerings outside the tree and shrub products, to include perennials, rose bushes and other plants, as well as flower seeds.
